About this story

discription

In the quiet of a moonlit night, a group of friends embarks on a hill-climbing adventure. But their plans take an otherworldly turn when a red stone falls from the sky, embedding itself within one of them. This seemingly ordinary stone holds cosmic secrets—a bridge to realms beyond. As the protagonist grapples with newfound powers, they encounter a strange man who reveals the stone’s true purpose: to mend or widen the Veil between worlds. Choices are made, oaths sworn, and destiny unfolds—a tale woven with magic, danger, and the promise of adventure.
